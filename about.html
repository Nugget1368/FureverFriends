About Us

We Make Tails Wag and Cats Purr

Furever Friends was founded by Vietnam War veteran Doe Joe, whose deep love for animals and commitment to community drove him to create a company dedicated to pet care. After returning from the war, Doe Joe saw a need for high-quality pet supplies that not only met the needs of pets but also supported their owners. In 2005, he established Furever Friends, aiming to provide innovative products that promote pet health and happiness.

With a focus on sustainability and ethical sourcing, Furever Friends quickly gained a reputation for excellence in the pet industry.
